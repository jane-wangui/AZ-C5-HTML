Curated African Basketry Items that Fit your Lifestyle!!!

We have curated unique and eco-friendly basketry items made by Africa Artisans.
See the sources of our basketry items below.

Rwanda

Artisans from Rwanda make the baskets from Sisal and Sweet Grass.
These are materials readily available and do not harm the Enviroment

Uganda

Also known as the Pearl of Africa, Uganda boasts of its large banana plantations.
The artisans use these readily available banana stems to make baskets.

Ghana

Artisans in Bolgatanga, Ghana, make the most versatile basketry items from the Elephant grass.
The most popular basketry item is the Accra shopping bag which is loved by clients all over the world.
